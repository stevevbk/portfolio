About Me

Certified Cloud Architect, I design and deploy high-performance, secure, and automated cloud infrastructures on Google Cloud Platform. My expertise covers containerization (Docker, Kubernetes), automation (Terraform, Jenkins), and the implementation of robust CI/CD pipelines. I have led projects ranging from web application deployment to the integration of AI solutions and chatbots, as well as the administration of Linux/Windows servers. Passionate about innovation, I am committed to turning cloud challenges into concrete and scalable solutions, serving the performance and security of information systems.
